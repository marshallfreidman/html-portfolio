The Best Movies According to Marshall

My top 3 movies of all-time

Lord of The Rings: The Return of The King

I think this is the best movie of all time. The music, scenery, acting, and plot all come together to make one of cinemas greatest fantasies.

Lost in Translation

This is a story about being lost, and finding oneself in a chance encounter in a strange world.

Gladiator

This is one of the movie greats with excellent action. It was one of my favorite movies growing up.
